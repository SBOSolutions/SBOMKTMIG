USER GUIDE WITH SCREENSHOTS

Enable the installment option: You can enable the installment option from the settings menu as shown in the image below.

Fill the required details: By providing the details like tenure, down payment, next payment date it will automatically calculate the installment amount and the installment dates.

After clicking on the "Confirm" button installments are calculated based on the information provided. The difference amount is calculated and added in the first installment as shown in the image below.

Creating an invoice:

The "View Installments" button will show all the installments calculated as per the information provided. And by clicking on the "Pay Down Payment" button you can pay the down payment.

Installments are displayed along with the "Payment Status" and a button "Pay" to pay that installment. As the installments are not paid the payment status initially will be "NOT PAID".

To start making payments you need to click on the "Confirm" button.

When you click on the button to pay the down payment then the down payment amount is automatically fetched.

When you click on the button to pay the installment then the installment amount is automatically fetched.

Payment generated for the down payment.

Payment generated for the first installment along with the difference amount.

When the installment payment is made then the payment status automatically changed to "PAID" and the rest of the payment status remain "NOT PAID".

Below image shows that all the installments have been paid and the payment status has been changed to "PAID".



If all the installments are not paid then it will reflect on the invoice as "Partially" paid.



If all the installments are fully paid then it will reflect on the invoice as "Paid".
